# P4 panel enclosure

This directory contains the editable Fusion archive and two printable halves for the 256×128 mm P4 HUB75 panel used by TTC Transit Screen.

- `p4-screen-case.f3d` — editable Fusion design archive
- `left-body.stl` — left enclosure half
- `right-body.stl` — right enclosure half

The combined STL bounds are approximately **260.5 × 142.5 × 36 mm**. The enclosure was test-printed in PLA. As a starting point, place each half flat on its broad back face and use:

- 0.2 mm layer height
- three walls/perimeters
- 20% infill
- supports only where your slicer's preview shows they are necessary

Use six M3×6 machine screws through the enclosure holes into the panel's threaded inserts. Start every screw loosely, align the panel, then tighten evenly. Do not overtighten the inserts.

This is a hobbyist design supplied as-is. Panel frames, connectors, print shrinkage, and printer calibration vary. Check cable clearance and test-fit all parts before final assembly. Improvements to tolerances, joining features, ventilation, or strain relief are welcome.
